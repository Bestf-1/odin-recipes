Jollof Rice Recipe

Sautee the paste and add the blended tomatos and paste with seasoning and broth to taste.

Add in washed rice and stir. Wait until done.

    Ingredients

  • Parboiled Rice
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chicken or Vegetable broth
  • Vegetable Oil
  • seasonings
  • Proteins (Chicken)

Steps

  1. Blend tomatoes, bell peppers, onions, garlic, and ginger into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a pot, saut√© the paste until thick and fragrant.
  3. Add tomato paste, broth, and seasonings. Cook until the sauce is rich and slightly reduced.
  4. Add rinsed rice and stir well to coat.
  5. Pour in enough broth or water to cover the rice, bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  6. Cover and simmer until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  7. Add optional proteins or vegetables and cook until done.
